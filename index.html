Odin Recipes ft Chicken

Assignment for CSS Foundations in The Odin Project Foundations course.

Approaching The Odin Project is surely a brave endeavor. Yet, strangely, all the recipes in this project involve chicken. In the wise words of Alanis Morrissette: "a little bit ironic, don't you think?".

I chose recipes that make you feel good when other methods don't work, like Chicken Soup, or recipes that have a personal significance, like the Pollo al Ajillo I shared with my partner on our first-date, or the Chicken Taynders, a tribute to Taylor Swift's favorite recipe, taught to me by my 15 year old Swiftie.

Anyways, here they are:

  • Chicken Soup
  • Strogonoff
  • Chicken Tenders
  • Pollo al Ajillo

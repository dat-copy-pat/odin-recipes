Chicken Soup pa'l almita

Marry Me Chicken Soup is the favorite cozy recipe of creamy, tangy chicken in stew form with rich flavors of sun-dried tomato, onion, garlic, chicken, cheese, and more. And it's the ideal bowl to tuck into on a quiet night at home with your special someone. The saying stands true: The way to the heart is through the stomach.

Ingredients

  • Sun-dried tomatoes:Julienne-cut sun-dried tomatoes in oil with herbs.
  • Aromatics:Onion, garlic, and tomato paste round out the rest of the flavor base.
  • Broth:Chicken broth and heavy cream combine to create a rich liquid.
  • Pasta:Medium shell pasta is cooked right in the soup.
  • Chicken:Julienne-like cut leftover roasted chicken.
  • Seasoning:Bring balance to the soup with basil, red pepper flakes, spinach, cream cheese, and Parmesan.

Steps

  1. Start by sautéing the onion and garlic in the oil from the sun-dried tomatoes.
  2. Then add in the tomatoes and tomato paste before pouring in broth, cream, and seasonings.
  3. Simmer this base together before adding the dried pasta to cook directly in the soup until al dente.
  4. Spinach and shredded rotisserie chicken go in toward the end to warm through.
  5. Add the cream cheese, Parmesan, and final garnishes for big spoonfuls of delicious flavor.

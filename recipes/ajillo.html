Chicken, garlic, creamy

The traditional Spanish Pollo al Ajillo is a classic home cooking dish for a perfect family dinner.

This is one of the most popular dishes on any tapas bar menu. For some extra sauce, add a chopped onion along with the garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 100 ml Olive oil
  • 1 large head of Garlic, cloves peeled and crushed
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 200ml Manzanilla sherry
  • Thyme and rosemary

Steps

  1. Leave the skin on. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large, heavy-based pan or casserole over a medium heat and add the chicken pieces..
  3. Fry the chicken on all sides until light golden; this should take about 15 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic and herbs and continue to cook until the chicken has taken on a dark brown colour, about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the sherry and stir to deglaze the bottom of the pan.
  6. Cook for another 5 minutes, until the sauce has reduced right down to almost nothing.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning and serve.

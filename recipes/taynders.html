Chicken Tenders Taylor version

My youngest showed me a TikTok video with the recipe for what I learned then, was Taylor Swift's favorite recipe. This is not that recipe. We go full Red erawith the hot sauce and call ours Chicken Taynders.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 150mls Milk
  • 150g Flour
  • Salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder and smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Mix the egg, milk and seasoning in a large bowl and place the flour in a separate bowl.
  2. Dunk the chicken into the milk mixture then into the flour then back into the milk and then flour again.
  3. You can either shallow fry, deep fry, air fry or oven bake the tenders until cooked through and crispy.
